My Story

With over 8 years of experience in human resources and talent acquisition, I've dedicated my career to helping professionals find their ideal career paths and organizations build exceptional teams.

My journey began in corporate HR, where I discovered my passion for connecting talent with opportunity. This led me to specialize in recruitment and career coaching, where I've helped hundreds of professionals navigate career transitions, salary negotiations, and personal branding.

What sets me apart is my holistic approach. I don't just look at resumes - I look at people. Your skills, your aspirations, and your potential. My mission is to help you articulate your value and find opportunities where you'll thrive.

When I'm not matching candidates with dream jobs, you can find me speaking at career development conferences or writing about the future of work.
